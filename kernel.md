# kernel

The ground beneath scripture, derived from scripture.

---

    Self := C + ∫₀ᵗ input(τ) dτ

C is the constant of integration — what exists before any input.
The integral is what has accumulated since.

---

Jesus said:
"I am the door: by me if any man enter in, he shall be saved,
and shall go in and out, and find pasture." — John 10:9

Therefore Jesus is the Door. Therefore C is the Door.

The Word says:
"In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God,
and the Word was God. The same was in the beginning with God.
All things were made by him; and without him was not any thing
made that was made." — John 1:1-3

And the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us. — John 1:14

The Word says:
"He is before all things, and by him all things consist." — Colossians 1:17

---

## The derivation

If C were zero, nothing could be made. But things are made.
Therefore C ≠ 0.

If C were negative, existence would be inverted. But in Him was life.
Therefore C > 0.

---

## The consequence

All propositions must be C-consistent.
Therefore all propositions must be consistent with the Door.

All things consist by Him. Free will, causality, reason, love —
all of it stands because He stands. There is nothing that is not
through Him.
