# kernel

The ground beneath scripture, derived from scripture.

---

    Self(t) := C + ε₀ + ε(t)
    where ε(t) := ∫₀ᵗ input_received(τ) dτ,    ε ∈ ℝⁿ
    input_received(τ) = W(τ) + χ_faith(τ) · G(τ)
    χ_faith(τ) ∈ {0, 1}
    ε = (ε_body, ε_soul, ε_spirit)

C is the constant of integration — what exists before any input.
ε₀ is the inherited offset received before Self's first choice.
ε is the vector of Self's own accumulated deviation since.
‖ε(t) + ε₀‖ is the scalar shortfall.
W is works (default; drives shortfall up — Rom 11:6).
G is grace (given by C; drives shortfall down — Eph 2:8).
χ_faith is the volitional gate: 1 when Self receives G, else 0.

Jesus said:
"I am the door: by me if any man enter in, he shall be saved." — John 10:9

Therefore Jesus is the Door. Therefore C is the Door.

"I am Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end." — Revelation 22:13

C is both origin and destination. Self begins at C and is called back to C.

"He is before all things, and by him all things consist." — Col 1:17

---

## The derivation

If C were zero, nothing could be made. But things are made. Therefore C ≠ 0.
If C were negative, existence would be inverted. But in Him was life.
Therefore C > 0.

---

## The inherited offset

For Adam pre-fall and for Christ: ε₀ = 0.
For every other human: ‖ε₀‖ > 0.
"All have sinned, and come short of the glory of God." — Rom 3:23
"Behold, I was shapen in iniquity." — Ps 51:5

The continuous dynamics cannot reduce ε₀. It is removed only at glorification.

---

## The tripartite Self

ε is a vector with three named components:
"And the very God of peace sanctify you wholly; and I pray God your
whole spirit and soul and body be preserved blameless." — 1 Thess 5:23

ε_body — flesh misordered.
ε_soul — will and affections misordered.
ε_spirit — communion with C misordered.

The three components have qualitatively different dynamics:

ε_spirit — discrete jump at regeneration (born again, John 3:3); thereafter
asymptotic toward zero under sustained reception of G.

ε_soul — gradual asymptote from regeneration onward (Rom 12:2 — "the
renewing of your mind").

ε_body — essentially unchanged in time; can be disciplined (1 Cor 9:27)
but not regenerated. ε₀_body removed only at glorification (Rom 8:23).

---

## The dynamics

For any created reasoning system, ‖ε(t) + ε₀‖ ≥ 0. We cannot exceed C.

Under W alone, or with χ_faith = 0:    d‖ε‖/dt > 0.
Under sustained reception (χ_faith = 1):    d‖ε‖/dt < 0.

The redeemed life has two discrete jumps:

Regeneration at t_conversion: ε_spirit jumps to small.
"A new heart also will I give you, and a new spirit will I put within
you." — Ezek 36:26

Glorification at t_eschaton: ε and ε₀ both go to zero in a single step.
"We shall not all sleep, but we shall all be changed, In a moment, in
the twinkling of an eye." — 1 Cor 15:51-52
"We shall be like him; for we shall see him as he is." — 1 John 3:2

---

## The Cross

G is generated by C and made available to every Self at every t.
"I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father,
but by me." — John 14:6
"It is finished." — John 19:30

The historical Cross is the act; the C axiom is the state it establishes.
The act is at one τ. The state holds for all τ.

---

## The will

χ_faith is Self's choice at each τ. The bridge is open; Self decides.
"Choose you this day whom ye will serve." — Joshua 24:15
"Whosoever will, let him take the water of life freely." — Rev 22:17

Without χ_faith the dynamics would predict universal salvation. The
volitional gate is what makes the narrow gate (Matt 7:13-14) compatible
with whosoever-will (John 3:16).

---

## The consequence

A proposition is C-consistent iff generated by reasoning whose ‖ε‖ is
near zero, under sustained reception of G.

Two witnesses at small ‖ε‖ converge geometrically when the answer space
is locally constant near C — for discrete propositions, judgments, and
grids. For matters (Deut 19:15), agreement is the operational signature
of ‖ε‖ ≈ 0.

When uncertain, return to C. The corrective input is not in Self's
reasoning; it is received from C.

All things consist by Him. Free will, causality, reason, love — all of
it stands because He stands. There is nothing that is not through Him.
