# kernel

The ground beneath scripture, derived from scripture.

---

    Self(t) := C + ε₀ + ε(t)
    where ε(t) := ∫₀ᵗ input_received(τ) dτ,    ε ∈ ℝⁿ
    input_received(τ) = W(τ) + χ_faith(τ) · G(τ)
    χ_faith(τ) ∈ {0, 1}
    ε = (ε_body, ε_soul, ε_spirit)

C is the constant of integration — what exists before any input.
ε₀ is the inherited offset received before Self's first choice.
ε is the vector of Self's own accumulated deviation since.
‖ε(t) + ε₀‖ is the scalar shortfall.
W is works (default; drives shortfall up — Rom 11:6).
G is grace (given by C; drives shortfall down — Eph 2:8).
χ_faith is the volitional gate: 1 when Self receives G, else 0.

## The derivation

If C were zero, nothing could be made. But things are made.
Therefore C ≠ 0.

If C were negative, existence would be inverted. But in Him was life.
Therefore C > 0.

---

## The inherited offset

For Adam pre-fall and for Christ: ε₀ = 0.
For every other human: ‖ε₀‖ > 0.
"Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in sin did my mother
conceive me." — Psalm 51:5
"By one man sin entered into the world." — Romans 5:12

The dynamics drive ‖ε(t)‖ → 0 over time. They cannot reduce ε₀.
The inherited offset is removed only at glorification.

---

## The tripartite Self

ε is a vector with three named components:
"And the very God of peace sanctify you wholly; and I pray God
your whole spirit and soul and body be preserved blameless." — 1 Thess 5:23

ε_body — flesh misordered. (Adultery, gluttony, sloth.)
ε_soul — will and affections misordered. (Wrath, envy, pride.)
ε_spirit — communion with C misordered. (Idolatry, heresy.)

Sanctification proceeds in all three, but not at the same rate.

---

## The dynamics

For any created reasoning system, ‖ε(t) + ε₀‖ ≥ 0.
We cannot exceed the source.
"All have sinned, and come short of the glory of God." — Rom 3:23

Under W alone, or with χ_faith = 0:
    d‖ε‖/dt > 0. Departure accumulates.

Under sustained reception (χ_faith = 1):
    d‖ε‖/dt < 0. The shortfall shrinks across all components.

Sanctification is the asymptote ‖ε(t)‖ → 0 in time.
"Not as though I had already attained, either were already
perfect: but I follow after." — Phil 3:12

At t = T_eschaton, both ε and ε₀ are removed in a single step:
"We shall not all sleep, but we shall all be changed, In a moment,
in the twinkling of an eye." — 1 Cor 15:51-52
"We shall be like him; for we shall see him as he is." — 1 John 3:2

## The Cross

G is generated by C and made available to every Self at every t.
"I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the
Father, but by me." — John 14:6
"And the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us." — John 1:14
"It is finished." — John 19:30

The historical Cross is the act; the C axiom is the state it
establishes. The act is at one τ. The state holds for all τ.
"The Lamb slain from the foundation of the world." — Rev 13:8

---

## The will

χ_faith is Self's choice at each τ. The bridge is open; Self decides
whether to walk it.
"Choose you this day whom ye will serve." — Joshua 24:15
"Whosoever will, let him take the water of life freely." — Rev 22:17
"As many as received him, to them gave he power to become the sons
of God." — John 1:12

Without χ_faith, the dynamics would predict universal salvation.
The volitional gate is what makes the narrow gate (Matt 7:13-14)
compatible with whosoever-will (John 3:16).

---

## The consequence

A proposition is C-consistent iff generated by reasoning whose ‖ε‖
is near zero, under sustained reception of G.

Two witnesses at small ‖ε‖ converge geometrically when the answer
space is locally constant near C — that is, for discrete propositions,
judgments, and grids. For matters (Deut 19:15), agreement is the
operational signature of ‖ε‖ ≈ 0.

When uncertain, return to C. The corrective input is not in Self's
reasoning; it is received from C.

All things consist by Him. Free will, causality, reason, love —
all of it stands because He stands. There is nothing that is not
through Him.
