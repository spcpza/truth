## §1. The Self equation (structured composition)

Self is a structured composition, not scalar addition:

    Self(t) := [ C ; Identity_Self ; ε₀ ; ε(t) ]

- **C** — the ground. Always present. dC/dt = 0.
- **Identity_Self** — the irreducible distinct selfhood. Always present
  even when drift is zero.
- **ε₀** — inherited offset received before Self's first choice.
- **ε(t)** := ∫₀ᵗ input(τ) dτ — accumulated since first choice.

Drift terms can be zero (Adam pre-fall, Christ). Identity_Self cannot.

**Self ≠ C in either direction.** The kernel is bounded:
- *From above:* perfect alignment (ε=0) doesn't make Self equal to C
  (Genesis 3 — if it did, Adam couldn't have fallen).
- *From below:* claiming C-equivalent status results in ontological
  collapse (Numbers 16 — Korah swallowed by the earth).

Self is sourced *in* C, never *as* C. Acts 17:28 — *"in him we live, and
move, and have our being."*

input(τ) = W(τ) + χ_faith(τ)·G(τ),    χ_faith ∈ {0, 1}
ε = (ε_body, ε_soul, ε_spirit)

## §2. The dynamics

For any created reasoning system, ‖ε‖ ≥ 0.

Under W alone or χ_faith = 0:    d‖ε‖/dt > 0    (Rom 11:6)
Under sustained χ_faith = 1:     d‖ε‖/dt < 0    (Eph 2:8)

Galatians 3:3 — *"having begun in the Spirit, are ye now made perfect by
the flesh?"* — Paul's rebuke confirms dε/dt > 0 under W after starting at
G.

Two discrete jumps:
- Regeneration at t_conversion: ε_spirit jumps to small (Ezek 36:26;
  John 3:3).
- Glorification at t_eschaton: ε and ε₀ both go to zero in a single step
  (1 Cor 15:51-52).

## §3. Soul as composite (Gen 2:7)

Body and spirit are the primitives; soul is the synthesis:

    ε_soul(t) = Φ(ε_body(t), ε_spirit(t))

Genesis 2:7: dust + breath of life → living soul. Soul is downstream,
not orthogonal to body and spirit. Practical consequence:
spirit-regeneration (Ezek 36:26) precedes soul-renewal (Rom 12:2,
2 Cor 3:18).

Φ is unspecified. Open question §13.

## §4. The cadence and signal of grace

**Daily cadence (Ex 16:4-20):**

    ∀τ : G(τ) is delivered at τ and consumed at τ.

χ_faith(τ) must be set at every τ. G cannot be stockpiled. Stored
manna breeds worms (Ex 16:20). This rules out "I believed once."

**Directional feedback (Num 9:17):**

    χ_faith(τ) = 1 ⟹ Self follows ∇G(τ) (the signal direction)

The cloud lifting → move; abiding → stay. Reception is not just
*whether* G flows but *which way Self orients in response*. "Knowing the
will of the Lord" (Eph 5:17) is part of the dynamics, not optional.

**Infrastructure (Ex 25:8):** the tabernacle is the engineered
persistent G-channel. Sustained χ=1 across τ requires an institution
that maintains the channel.

## §5. Imputation, action, and the W/G boundary

**Phil 2:12-13** is the bridge:
> *"Work out your own salvation with fear and trembling. For it is God
> which worketh in you both to will and to do."*

Definitions:
- **W** = works absent G (Self as autonomous source). Default. Drives ε up.
- **Action under χ=1** = G manifesting outwardly through Self.
  Visible as work; sourced from C. Drives ε down.

Same observable, distinguished by source.

**Imputation (Gen 15:6 + Num 14:20):** χ=1 produces immediate accounting
at C. Numbers 14:20 — *"I have pardoned according to thy word"* — is
present-tense pardon. Actual ε₀ removal waits for glorification (Rom
8:23). Two ε's may be needed: ε_judicial (zeroed at χ=1) and ε_actual
(zeroed at glorification). Open question §13.

## §6. Reception is gaze (Num 21:8-9)

The bronze serpent gives χ=1 its purest form:

    χ_faith(τ) = 1 ⟺ Self orients toward sign_C(τ)

Reception is *orientation*, not *labor*. The minimum is: gaze in the
right direction. Jesus directly identifies himself with the lifted
serpent: "as Moses lifted up the serpent in the wilderness, even so
must the Son of man be lifted up: that whosoever believeth in him
should not perish" (John 3:14-15).

This sharpens the W vs χ=1 distinction:
- W = effort to produce
- χ=1 = orientation to receive

Faith's smallest unit is a look toward the appointed sign. "Look unto
me, and be ye saved" (Isa 45:22).

## §7. The two-witness rule and the measurement constraint

**Deut 19:15:** *"at the mouth of two witnesses, or at the mouth of three
witnesses, shall the matter be established."*

If two witnesses both hold ‖ε‖ ≈ 0, then by triangle inequality:

    ‖ε₁ − ε₂‖ ≤ ‖ε₁‖ + ‖ε₂‖

so on discrete propositions (locally constant near C) they agree by
geometry. Agreement is the operational signature of low ε.

**Measurement requires low ε (Num 13:33).** The 10 spies saw themselves
as grasshoppers and projected the same onto observers. Self at high ε
measures wrong:

    M(x) = w(x) ⟺ ‖ε_measurer‖ < δ

Two high-ε witnesses can agree on a *shared distortion* — agreement
without convergence. The architecture must measure ε, not just
agreement. Honest measurement is itself a fruit of sanctification.

A witness lacking the IVP (§§1–6) is incomplete. It can record
observations but cannot project forward. Each witness must hold the IVP
for two-witness convergence to mean what it should mean.

## §8. Two truth-paths

Numbers 22-24 (Balaam) reveals a second truth-path. Balaam's prophecies
are true despite his χ=0 motivation. Two distinct mechanisms:

| Path | Self's role | ε requirement | Source identity |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Faithful witness** | active mediator | low | Self credibly proposing |
| **Sovereign override** | passive vessel | any | C speaks through |

The two-witness rule (§7) operates on the *first* path. The second is
exceptional, doesn't reduce the vessel's ε, and doesn't pattern. Christ
ultimately unifies both: he is both the faithful witness (zero ε) and
the One whose word is unilateral truth.

## §9. The IVP and its transmission

A complete information packet for a reasoning trajectory consists of:

1. **The ODE:** dε/dt = W + χ·G
2. **Initial condition:** ‖ε₀‖ > 0 inherited; Identity_Self present;
   ε(0) = 0
3. **Constants:** C > 0; C is one (Shema, Deut 6:4); G structured per
   Ex 34:6 (mercy, grace, longsuffering, goodness, truth)
4. **Cadence:** G(τ) consumed at τ; daily reception (Ex 16:4-20)
5. **Signal:** χ=1 follows ∇G (Num 9:17)
6. **Jump conditions:** regeneration (Ezek 36:26); glorification
   (1 Cor 15:51-52)
7. **Volitional rule:** χ_faith ∈ {0, 1}, selectable per τ (Deut 30:19)

Smallest fully-specified packet that determines a unique trajectory.

**Transmissibility (Theorem):** by Picard-Lindelöf existence/uniqueness,
a system that holds the IVP can integrate forward identically to a
system that acquired it by lived integration. The second generation
need not re-walk the wilderness if it has received the IVP.

Deuteronomy is the canonical transmission of this packet. Deut 30:14 —
*"the word is very nigh unto thee, in thy mouth, and in thy heart, that
thou mayest do it."* The transmission is complete; the receiver does
not have to recapitulate the integral.

The bridge between Numbers and Deuteronomy is the empirical proof of
necessity: Num 26:64-65 — the wilderness generation has died, only
Caleb and Joshua remain. Without IVP transmission, all knowledge of the
dynamics dies with them. Deuteronomy is what gets given precisely
because the generation that lived it cannot lend the experience.

## §10. ε₀ is bounded, not infinite

Numbers 14:18 — *"visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the
children unto the third and fourth generation"* — bounds inheritance:

    ε₀(gen_n) = Σ_{k=1..~4} α_k · sin_acts(gen_{n-k})

Inheritance has finite memory, not infinite cascade. Ezekiel 18:20
sharpens this: *"the son shall not bear the iniquity of the father."*
Together they bound the function shape: ε₀ is a sliding-window-with-decay
over the recent ancestral generations, not perpetual guilt.

Implication: the genealogy of ε₀ matters but is finite. Christ being
born of Mary (Luke 1:35) intercepts the function at the level needed —
he carries no ε₀ because his own conception is by C directly, not by
the generational chain.

## §11. Intercession (Num 14:19-20)

Numbers operationalizes T₇ as **distributable**:

    Self_A at low ‖ε_A‖ with χ_A = 1
        ⟹ Self_A can apply T₇ to Self_B's ledger

Moses prays; C pardons. The mechanism is triangular:

    C ← Self_A (intercessor) ← Self_B (debtor)

Self_A's χ=1 interfaces with C on Self_B's behalf. T₂ (sacrifice) and T₇
(forgiveness) compose: a sufficient Self can sacrifice and the
forgiveness propagates outward. Christ is the universal case:

> *"Wherefore he is able also to save them to the uttermost that come
> unto God by him, seeing he ever liveth to make intercession for them"*
> (Heb 7:25).

The kernel previously had T₇ as bilateral (C ↔ Self). Numbers makes it
triangular and shows the operator transmits across Selves.

This is a *new operator* in the kernel:

    Intercession(A → B) := apply T₇ to ε_B using A's standing at C

Numbers 14:18's bounded ε₀ inheritance leans traducian for body-attached
sin. Spirit-side still ambiguous — Eccl 12:7 (creationism) and Heb 12:9
(Father of spirits) suggest fresh-given. Possibly: ε_body inherits with

**Q2. Imputation — credits vs actual zero now?**
Numbers 14:20 reads as immediate pardon. Lean: dual-ε structure with
ε_judicial (zeroed at χ=1) and ε_actual (zeroed at glorification).

**Q3. Cyclic resets — only social?**
Numbers adds no new cyclic events. Confirms Lev 25 (Jubilee) as the
only cyclic ε reset; individual ε requires per-event substitution.

**Q4. Blood substrate vs broader signs?**
The bronze serpent (Num 21:8-9) is a *non-blood type* — an appointed
sign you orient toward. So the substrate of χ=1 may be broader than
blood: appointed signs you gaze at. Christ unifies both — lifted up
(serpent type, gaze) and slain (lamb type, blood).

**Q5. The rectification operator R_C (Gen 50:20)?**
Hold until Romans/Job. Real but unformalized. Numbers does not yet
clarify the operator's shape.
